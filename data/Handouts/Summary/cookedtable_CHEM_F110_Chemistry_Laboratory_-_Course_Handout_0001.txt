
Course: CHEM F110 Chemistry Laboratory
Summary:
The course encompasses various experiments, including:
Acetonate complex synthesis (Lab 5)
Synthesis of glucosazone in the 2nd cycle (Lab 6)
Determination of activation energy using the hydrogen peroxide-potassium iodide clock reaction (Lab 7)
pH curve determination in an acid-base titration (Lab 8)
Estimation of copper through Iodometry (Lab 9)
Determination of unknown strengths of strong and weak acid solutions via conductometric titration (Lab 9).
