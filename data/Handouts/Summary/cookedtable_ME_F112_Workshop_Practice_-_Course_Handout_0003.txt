
Course: ME F112 Workshop Practice
Summary:
The ME F112 Workshop Practice course evaluation includes a Mid-Semester Exam with a duration of 60 minutes and a weightage of 15%, scheduled for 16/03 from 9:30 AM to 11:00 AM. The Comprehensive Exam has a duration of 120 minutes and a weightage of 25%, with the date and time to be announced later. Classroom assessment and regular classwork contribute 10% each to the final grade, and the Laboratory Practical has a weightage of 40%. The nature of the components varies, with the Mid-Semester Exam and Comprehensive Exam being closed book (CB), and the Classroom assessment, regular classwork, and Laboratory Practical being open book (OB).
