
Course: BIO F110_BIOLOGY_LABORATORY
Summary:
BIO F110 Biology Laboratory at Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani Hyderabad Campus, in the second semester 2023-24, is an introductory course focused on practical applications of biology. Under the guidance of Dr. Kumar Pranav Narayan and a team of instructors, students perform experiments covering microscopy, macromolecule quantification, chlorophyll estimation, cell cycle parameters, hematology, and DNA quantification. The course aims to foster an appreciation for biological concepts and develop essential laboratory skills.
