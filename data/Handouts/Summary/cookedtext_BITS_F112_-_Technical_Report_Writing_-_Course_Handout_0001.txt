
Course: BITS F112 - Technical Report Writing
Summary:
The BITS F112 Technical Report Writing course, instructed by Pranesh Bhargava with support from Aruna Lolla and A.K. Jayesh, aims to enhance students' abilities in composing technical reports and delivering academic presentations. The focus includes writing project proposals, letters of purpose, and detailed reports. The recommended textbook is "Cambridge Academic English (Advanced)" by Martin Hewings and Craig Thaine, supplemented by various reference books and online resources provided throughout the course.
