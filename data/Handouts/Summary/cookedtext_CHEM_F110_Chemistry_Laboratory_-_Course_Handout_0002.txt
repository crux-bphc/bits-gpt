
Course: CHEM F110 Chemistry Laboratory
Summary:
The evaluation for CHEM F110 Chemistry Laboratory includes components such as lab performance, calculation, and data interpretation. The mid-semester quiz and lab exam are integral parts of the assessment. Additionally, factors like attendance, punctuality, laboratory conduct, and record maintenance contribute to the overall evaluation, with a maximum of 15 marks allocated for these aspects. All nine experiments will be assessed in the lab, and the best eight performances will be considered for the final evaluation. This comprehensive approach ensures a thorough assessment of students' practical skills and understanding of the experiments conducted during the course.
