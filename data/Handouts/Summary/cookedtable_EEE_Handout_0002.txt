
Course: Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE)
Summary:
The course evaluation includes daily interaction and assessment, with quizzes contributing 10% (30 marks) to the final grade. A mid-term exam, lasting 40 minutes, holds a weightage of 20% (60 marks), while the comprehensive examination, lasting 90 minutes, contributes 30% (90 marks). The final examination, lasting 3 hours, holds the highest weightage at 40% (120 marks). The dates for quizzes are daily during lecture sessions, the mid-term exam will be announced a week before in class, and the final exam will align with the timetable. Marks distribution and weightage are indicated for each component (OB: Open Book, CB: Closed Book).
