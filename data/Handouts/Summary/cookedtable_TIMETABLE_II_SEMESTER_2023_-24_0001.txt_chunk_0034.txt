
Here are the summaries of the courses:
Instrumental Methods of Analysis (PHA F313):
Conducted by A SAJELI BEGUM, Instrumental Methods of Analysis (PHA F313) consists of 3 lecture hours and 1 practical hour per week, totaling 4 course units. Classes are scheduled on Tuesdays and Thursdays, with a mid-semester exam on 14th March and a comprehensive exam on 14th May in the afternoon session. Practical sessions are conducted by A Sajeli Begum on Tuesdays.
Pharmacology II (PHA F341):
Taught by ONKAR KULKARNI, Pharmacology II (PHA F341) offers 2 lecture hours and 1 practical hour per week, amounting to 3 course units. Classes occur on Mondays and Wednesdays. The mid-semester exam is scheduled for 16th March, and the comprehensive exam is on 20th May in the afternoon session. Practical sessions are conducted by Onkar Kulkarni on Tuesdays.
Medicinal Chemistry II (PHA F342):
Under the instruction of P YOGEESWARI, Medicinal Chemistry II (PHA F342) comprises 2 lecture hours and 1 practical hour per week, making up 3 course units. Classes take place on Tuesdays and Thursdays. The mid-semester exam is set for 11th March, and the comprehensive exam is on 6th May in the forenoon session. Practical sessions are conducted by P Yogeeswari on Wednesdays.
Forensic Pharmacy (PHA F343):
Led by BALRAM GHOSH, Forensic Pharmacy (PHA F343) entails 2 lecture hours per week, totaling 2 course units. Classes are held on Tuesdays and Thursdays. The mid-semester exam is scheduled for 14th March, and the comprehensive exam is on 13th May in the forenoon session.
Natural Drugs (PHA F344):
Instructed by A SAJELI BEGUM, Natural Drugs (PHA F344) includes 2 lecture hours and 1 practical hour per week, totaling 3 course units. Classes take place on Mondays and Wednesdays. The mid-semester exam is set for 13th March, and the comprehensive exam is on 11th May in the forenoon session. Practical sessions are conducted by A Sajeli Begum on Thursdays.
Laboratory Project (PHA F366):
The course Laboratory Project (PHA F366) is available for 3 course units, with no specified lecture or practical hours. It is instructed by ABHIJEET RAJENDRA JOS.
Design Project (PHA F376):
Conducted by ABHIJEET RAJENDRA JOS, Design Project (PHA F376) offers 3 course units without specified lecture or practical hours.
Pharma Management & Quality Control (PHA F413):
Taught by V V KRISHNA VENUGANTI, Pharma Management & Quality Control (PHA F413) consists of 3 lecture hours per week, making up 3 course units. Classes occur on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. The mid-semester exam is scheduled for 15th March, and the comprehensive exam is on 16th May in the forenoon session.
