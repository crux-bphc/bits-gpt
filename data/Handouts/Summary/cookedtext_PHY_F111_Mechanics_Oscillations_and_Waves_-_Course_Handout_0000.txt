
Summary:
The course "Mechanics, Oscillations & Waves" (Course No.: PHY F111) at Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani Hyderabad Campus, in the second semester of 2023-2024, is detailed in the course handout. The instructor in charge is Subhadeep Roy, and additional instructors are listed. The course covers fundamental physics topics, including polar coordinates, angular momentum, central force motion, harmonic oscillator, coupled oscillations, waves, and wave equations. The objective is to develop physics problem-solving skills and a deep conceptual understanding. The recommended textbooks include "An Introduction to Mechanics" by D. Kleppner and R. Kolenkow and "Vibrations and Waves" by Anthony P. French. Various reference books are also listed to provide a solid foundation for advanced physics courses.
