
Course: CHEM F110 Chemistry Laboratory
Summary:
Make-up Policy: No makeup sessions will be granted.
Notice: Important information will be communicated via the Chemistry Department Notice Board and/or CMS. A lab manual and a white lab coat, along with appropriate footwear covering the entire feet, are mandatory.
Academic Honesty and Integrity Policy: Students are expected to maintain academic honesty and integrity throughout the semester, with no tolerance for any form of academic dishonesty.
Final Grading: The overall performance of students in each component outlined in item no. 6 will determine the final grading. The instructor-in-charge oversees the Chemistry Laboratory. This information ensures clarity on policies, communication channels, and expectations for students participating in the chemistry laboratory course.
