
Course: BITS F112 - Technical Report Writing
Summary:
The BITS F112 Technical Report Writing course covers diverse methods and sources for data collection, including interviewing, surveying, observation, testing, and utilizing published information. It delves into the effective use of illustrations within reports, offering guidance on incorporating graphics and avoiding misrepresentation. Additionally, the course explores oral presentation skills, encompassing planning, speech notes, slide design, and techniques for overcoming stage fright, ensuring students comprehend the essential dos and don'ts of technical presentations.
