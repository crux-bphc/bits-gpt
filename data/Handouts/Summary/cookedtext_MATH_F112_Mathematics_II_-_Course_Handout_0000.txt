
Summary:
The text pertains to the course "MATHEMATICS-II" (Course No.: MATH F112) at Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani Hyderabad Campus, in the second semester of 2023-2024. The instructor-in-charge is Nijjwal Karak, with a team of additional instructors. The course introduces Linear Algebra and the Theory of Functions of Complex Variables and their applications. Topics include linear equations, eigenvalues, eigenvectors, vector spaces, linear transformations, complex variables, analyticity, elementary functions, integration, Taylor and Laurent series expansions, and calculus of residues. Required textbooks are "Linear Algebra with Applications" by G. Williams and "Complex Variables and Applications" by R.V. Churchill and J.W. Brown, with additional reference books listed. The text emphasizes environmental responsibility, discouraging unnecessary printing.
