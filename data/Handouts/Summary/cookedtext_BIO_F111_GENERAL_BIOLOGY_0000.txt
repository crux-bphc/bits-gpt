
Course: BIO F111_GENERAL_BIOLOGY
Summary:
BIO F111 General Biology at BITS Pilani explores living systems, biological compounds, genetics, and DNA technology. The course aims to connect biology with diverse disciplines, emphasizing its relevance. The textbook is "Campbell Essential Biology with Physiology," and reference books include "Concepts in Biology" and "Biology: Concept and Application." The course focuses on instilling a comprehensive understanding of biology's influence on daily life and various disciplines.
