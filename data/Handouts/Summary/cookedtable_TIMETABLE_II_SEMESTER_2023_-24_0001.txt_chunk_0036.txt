
Here are the summaries of the courses:
Introduction to Astro & Astrophysics (PHY F215):
Taught by SUBHASH N. KARBELKAR, Introduction to Astro & Astrophysics (PHY F215) consists of 3 lecture hours per week, totaling 3 course units. Classes are scheduled on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. The mid-semester exam is on 11th March, and the comprehensive exam is on 7th May in the forenoon session.
Electromagnetic Theory II (PHY F241):
Conducted by SASHIDEEP GUTTI, Electromagnetic Theory II (PHY F241) comprises 3 lecture hours and 1 practical hour per week, amounting to 4 course units. Classes occur on Mondays and Wednesdays. The mid-semester exam is set for 15th March, and the comprehensive exam is on 16th May in the forenoon session. Practical sessions are led by Sashideep Gutti on Fridays.
