CHEM F110 CHEMISTRY LABORATORY

1. Course Description: This course is based on laboratory experiments in different fields of chemistry. The course is conducted for first year students.

2. Scope and Objective: The main objective of this course is to educate the students with different aspects of experiments in chemistry. The students will carry out a set of experiments that will expose them to various experimental techniques like preparation of standard solution, chemical synthesis, filtrations, recrystallization, and operating of different scientific equipment for collecting data and analysis. Experiments in Organic and Inorganic chemistry will include synthesis and analysis of different compounds. Experiments in Physical chemistry will include pH metry, conductometry, spectrophotometry and chemical kinetics experiments.

3. Text Book(s): Chemistry Laboratory Manual

4. Reference Book: Reference sources for each experiment will be specified as necessary.

5. Course Plan: Students will perform selected experiments in Organic, Inorganic & Physical chemistry
